Tribute to Albert Einstein

Celebrating the legacy of a scientific genius

Biography

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics (alongside quantum mechanics). He is best known for his mass-energy equivalence formula, E = mc2.

Major Achievements

  • Nobel Prize in Physics (1921) for his explanation of the photoelectric effect
  • Developed the Theory of Relativity
  • Formulated the famous equation E = mc2
  • Contributed to the development of quantum theory

Famous Quotes

"Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving." - Albert Einstein

"Imagination is more important than knowledge." - Albert Einstein
